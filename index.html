Grimoire Games Summer Camp will be a fun and encouraging space for kids to play Dungeons and Dragons! Learning how to play Dungeons and Dragons can be a daunting task for kids of any age, and this camp will make it accessible and easy for everyone! We will provide a space and the materials needed for kids to make friends and have fun, while learning problem solving skills, engaging in mental math, and practicing structured creative play.
